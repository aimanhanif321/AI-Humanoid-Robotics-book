# Welcome to the AI-Humanoid Robotics Book!

This book is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of Physical AI, with a special focus on humanoid robotics. Whether you're a student, researcher, or enthusiast, you'll find foundational concepts and advanced topics to deepen your knowledge.

## What you will learn:

- The foundations of Physical AI and embodied intelligence.
- Robotics fundamentals, including sensors, actuators, and control.
- ROS 2 architecture and robot modeling with URDF.
- Digital twin concepts and interactive simulation environments.
- NVIDIA Isaac Sim for AI training and synthetic data generation.
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems.
- The hardware ecosystem for Physical AI.

Start exploring the chapters to embark on your journey into AI-Humanoid Robotics!
